The Problem

Self.ai is a non-profit organization whose mission is to promote healthy minds and lifestyles as well as mindfulness about how our daily lives affect our mental health. This initiative was started by high-school students who are deeply passionate about mental health. Many people suffer in silence and donâ€™t open up about their feelings in fear of judgment. Solution

We built an app that uses artificial intelligence to analyze your emotions through your voice and facial features. Our inspiration was a personal experience that changed us as people, this project is very close to our hearts. We hope our app serves as a companion that allows people to let out all their feelings when they are feeling down or self-critical.






Features






Facial Scanner

Our software reads key aspects of faces to judge your perception

Speech and Tone analysis

Using our NLP algorithm, Self.ai analyzes user audio to determine your wellbeing

Sentiment Interpretation

By combining our facial, audio, and text analysis, Self.ai creates an summary of your mental state for you and your support group
